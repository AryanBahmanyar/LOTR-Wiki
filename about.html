About the Author

Aryan Bahmanyar is a student at Seneca College, where he is studying Computer Programming & Analysis. He made this website about LOTR for an assignment in his WEB222 course. Prior to enrolling in the course, Aryan had some experience with HTML and CSS on his own time, but he is always striving to learn more and continue to improve. Although Aryan is not a professional writer or web developer, he hopes that this website will do the LOTR franchise justice, and more importantly, showcase his knowledge of front-end web development.
